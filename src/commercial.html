About Bella Rosa Village Commercial

Bella Rosa Village, a mixed-use development exceeding R300 million, was completed in 2009 by Propergation Management Services, Power Developments, and Atterbury Cape. It comprises approximately 13,500m2 of A-grade offices, 210 residential units, and 21 single residential erven. Located on Durban Road in Tyger Valley, it offers excellent visibility and easy access from major highways, close to Cape Town CBD, the airport, and winelands. The architecture blends traditional Italian village charm with hi-tech design and contemporary finishes, set in an attractive landscaped environment with water features and trees. It is a popular office node for top businesses. A state-of-the-art lifestyle centre provides sporting and social amenities including a boardroom, auditorium, juice bar, restaurant, braai facilities, training pool, squash courts, and a fully equipped gymnasium with an in-house bio-kinetic instructor and personal trainer. Security is paramount, featuring supervised 24-hour CCTV, patrol, front gate security, manned access control portals, and convenient, secure open and basement parking. Limited office and retail opportunities are available.
