Bella Rosa Village offers a secure and tranquil residential lifestyle with direct access from Durban Road, within walking distance of Tyger Valley Centre, adjacent to High Street Shopping Village, and close to schools, medical care facilities, and major highways.

The village is designed for privacy and space, featuring cobbled walkways, indigenous blooms, trees, bustling bird life, and water features.

Security is paramount with 24-hour patrol, front gate security, and commanding portals for easy access control.

A state-of-the-art lifestyle centre caters to all sporting and social amenities, including a reception, boardroom, lounges, mini-theatre, full kitchen, braai facilities, squash courts, a fully-equipped gymnasium with an in-house bio-kinetic instructor and personal trainer, and a children's swimming pool.

Only 6 prime erven are still for sale, offering the opportunity to build a dream house.

Bella Rosa Village was designed by Dennis Moss Partnership in association with EBESA Architects for developers Propergation Estates, Atterbury, and Power Developments.
