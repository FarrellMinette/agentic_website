Welcome to Bella Rosa Village

Bella Rosa Village is a prestigious mixed-use development offering a unique blend of A-grade offices, luxurious residential units, and prime erven. Completed in 2009, this R300 million development seamlessly combines traditional Italian village charm with modern design and contemporary finishes. Located on Durban Road in Tyger Valley, it boasts excellent visibility, easy access to major highways, and proximity to Cape Town CBD, the airport, and the winelands.

Experience a secure and tranquil lifestyle with paramount security features, attractive landscaped environments, and a state-of-the-art lifestyle centre. The centre provides a comprehensive range of sporting and social amenities, including a fully equipped gymnasium, training pools, squash courts, conferencing facilities, and dining options. Bella Rosa Village is a popular destination for top businesses and discerning residents seeking quality and convenience.
